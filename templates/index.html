Music Genre Classification

This small web app uses a neural network to predict the genre of a song you upload.
The network can predict one of ten genres:

  • Pop
  • Metal
  • Disco
  • Blues
  • Reggae
  • Classical
  • Rock
  • Hip-hop
  • Country
  • Jazz
This app currently supports .wav (recommended), .ogg, and .flac
Mp3 files are not currently supported.
